Allan + Avocado

My name is Allan, my nickname “Abacate” or Avocado. In text I explain why the nickname. I’m from Brazil.

In 2017 I decided to do my first backpacking through South America and to record the moments I decided to buy a camera, a Fujifilm.
When I started backpacking in the first country of Bolivia to go to the Salar de Uyuni, the biggest salt desert in the world, I try shoot the first photo,
the Photo was basically Black, so I try to understand why. I couldn't take any pictures that day and I thought the camera was broken.

I went looking for a video tutorial on YouTube and found out what was happening, so as I was doing nothing at the moment I decided to watch some other
videos about basic photography and understanding how a camera works. From that moment I just fell in love with photography and after that I never
stopped photographing.

My nickname started at school, but I'm not sure why... that's all
